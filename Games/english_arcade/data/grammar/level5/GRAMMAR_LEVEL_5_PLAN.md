# Grammar Level 5 Plan

## Overview
This document outlines the grammar topics, lessons, and learning objectives for Grammar Level 5.
Level 5 focuses on advanced grammar concepts including conditional sentences, reported speech, passive voice, and complex sentence structures.

---

## Structure
- **Location**: `Games/english_arcade/data/grammar/level5/`
- **Lesson Modules**: `Games/english_arcade/modes/lessons_5/`
- **Data Files**: JSON files containing example sentences and vocabulary
