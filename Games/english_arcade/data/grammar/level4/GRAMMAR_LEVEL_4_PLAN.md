## Grammar Topics

### Topic 1: Possessive Adjectives, Possessive Pronouns, & Object Pronouns
**Objectives**:
- Understand possessive adjectives (my, your, his, her, its, our, their)
- Learn possessive pronouns (mine, yours, his, hers, ours, theirs)
- Master object pronouns (me, you, him, her, it, us, them)
- Distinguish and use all three correctly in context

**Lessons**:
- grammar_lesson_pronouns_comprehensive.js

**JSON Data Files**:
- possessive_adjectives_pronouns_objects.json
