# Grammar Level 6 Plan

## Overview
This document outlines the grammar topics, lessons, and learning objectives for Grammar Level 6.
Level 6 focuses on advanced upper-intermediate grammar concepts including perfect progressives, advanced conditionals, and sophisticated structures.
